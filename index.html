Bio

Through the various roles in his career, Steve has more than 15 years in the Information Technology and Information Security space, including nine years in the Healthcare sector alone.

Steve helped create and evolve the original Security Operations Team into a Multi-Team Security Operations Center (SOC) at one of the largest non-profit healthcare systems in the United States. His background in Active Directory, Windows, Azure, Intune, and SCCM separates him from the crowd in the security field. Understanding the core infrastructure and deployment methodologies allows him to identify opportunities outside the normal Pentest, ensuring a stable and secure environment. Identifying new ways to solve difficult problems, Steve strives to learn something new every day and enjoys sharing his knowledge with others in hopes they will find something useful..

Steve now works as a security consultant, helping customers around the globe secure their environments. When not securing large Enterprises, he is an adjunct professor at a University teaching the next generation of security professionals and is a staff member for the local security conference, B-Sides, providing further opportunities to develop those within his community.
